Task 2: What are HTML Entities? List out 5 Commonly used HTML Entities.

solution:

- HTML entities are special codes used to display reserved characters or symbols in web pages.
Here are five commonly used HTML entities:

- codes or placeholders used in HTML to display characters that have special meanings in HTML or that are reserved for specific purposes.


1. `&lt;` for less than (<).
2. `&gt;` for greater than (>).
3. `&amp;` for ampersand (&).
4. `&quot;` for double quote (").
5. `&apos;` for single quote (').
