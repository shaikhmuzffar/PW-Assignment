Task 5:Define favicon and give an illustrative example.

solution:

- A favicon, short for "favorite icon," is a small, often square-shaped icon associated with a website or webpage.
- Favicons are displayed in web browsers' address bars, bookmarks, tabs, and other places to visually represent a website and provide a recognizable and branded identity.
- They are typically saved as ICO (icon) files but can also be in other formats like PNG or GIF.
