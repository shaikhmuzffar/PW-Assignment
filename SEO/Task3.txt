Task3: What are the Benefits of using meta tags.

solution:

several benefits of Meta tags:

1. Improved SEO: Meta tags, like meta descriptions and meta keywords, help search engines understand and rank your page better in search results.

2. Enhanced User Experience: Meta descriptions provide a brief summary of your page, helping users decide if it's relevant before clicking.

3. Better Social Sharing: Meta tags like Open Graph tags enable proper formatting when your content is shared on social media, making it more appealing.

4. Accurate Page Indexing: Meta robots tags can instruct search engines on how to crawl and index your content correctly.

5. Faster Page Loading: Some meta tags, like meta refresh or meta viewport, optimize page loading and display on different devices.
